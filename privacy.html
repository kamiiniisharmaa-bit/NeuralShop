Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters at NeuralShop. We are committed to protecting your personal data.

Last updated: June 17, 2026

1. Data We Collect

NeuralShop collects information to provide, improve, and personalise your shopping experience. This includes:

  • Account data: name, email, shipping address, payment details.
  • Behavioural data: browsing history, search queries, product interactions, and AI‑generated preferences.
  • Technical data: device type, IP address, browser version, and cookies.
Neural Insight: We use this data to train our AI models and deliver hyper‑personalised recommendations — but never share your identity with third parties.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data powers the NeuralShop experience. We use it to:

  • Curate product recommendations and AI‑driven deals.
  • Process transactions and manage your orders.
  • Improve our algorithms, user interfaces, and customer support.
  • Send you personalised offers, updates, and marketing (with your consent).

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell your personal data. However, we may share it with:

  • Service providers: payment processors, delivery partners, and cloud hosting (all GDPR/CCPA compliant).
  • Legal authorities: if required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Business transfers: in case of a merger or acquisition.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyse traffic, and personalise content. You can manage your preferences in your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures, including encryption, access controls, and regular audits, to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.
  • Opt‑out of AI‑based profiling (subject to limited functionality).
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@neuralshop.ai.

GDPR & CCPA compliant: NeuralShop respects your privacy rights under applicable laws. We respond to all requests within 30 days.

7. AI & Automated Decision‑Making

Our AI systems may make automated decisions (e.g., product ranking, dynamic pricing). You have the right to request human intervention or contest decisions by contacting our support team.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on the Platform.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or data requests, please email privacy@neuralshop.ai or use our support form.
